1. Lack of Checkmate Detection

      The current implementation does not accurately detect a checkmate scenario, allowing the game to continue indefinitely after a winning position is achieved.

    2. Lack of Forced Check Defense

      After a pawn is promoted, the game does not correctly verify whether the promotion results in a check or checkmate, affecting the overall gameplay integrity.

    3. Check After Promotion Not Implemented

      After a pawn is promoted, the game does not correctly verify whether the promotion results in a check or checkmate, affecting the overall gameplay integrity.

    4. Incorrect Castling Logic

      The current castling logic fails to detect when a square between the king and rook is under check, allowing castling in scenarios where it should be invalid according to chess rules.

    5. Piece Movement Bug

      Intermittent issues with piece movement have been observed, where pieces stop responding or moving after a certain time during the game. This impacts the fluidity of the game experience.
